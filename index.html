Meet the Heroes

Fantastic Four

Marvel's first superhero family, the Fantastic Four, are a team of explorers and adventurers transformed by cosmic radiation. Led by the brilliant Reed Richards (Mr. Fantastic), the team also includes Sue Storm (Invisible Woman), Johnny Storm (Human Torch), and Ben Grimm (The Thing).

X-Men

The X-Men are a group of mutants, led by Professor Charles Xavier, who fight for peace and coexistence in a world that often fears and hates them. Each member has a unique mutation, from Wolverine's claws and healing factor to Cyclops' optic blasts and Storm's weather control.

The Avengers

The Avengers are Earth's Mightiest Heroes, a team assembled to take on the world's greatest threats. Formed by Nick Fury, they include iconic members like Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Black Widow, and Hulk.

Spiderman

As a solo hero, Spider-Man fights crime across New York City, balancing his life as Peter Parker with his duties as the web-slinging protector. Despite often working alone, Spider-Man is known for his wit, heart, and resilience.
